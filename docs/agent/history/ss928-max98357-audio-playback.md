### skills/ss928-max98357-audio-playback

- Goal: preserve the verified flow from user MP3 to SS928 MPP sample-compatible audio, board upload, 40Pin I2S pinmux, and MAX98357 speaker playback.
- Key files: `skills/ss928-max98357-audio-playback/SKILL.md`; `scripts/prepare_audio.py` generates `audio_chn0.aac`, 48k/16bit/stereo PCM, and `play_hint.txt`; UI metadata is in `agents/openai.yaml`.
- Verified path: local `test.mp3` was about 9 seconds at 16 kHz mono. After conversion and upload to `/root/max98357_i2s_test`, running the Pin12 `I2S_BCLK`, Pin38 `I2S_WS`, Pin40 `I2S_SD_TX`, and Pin7 `I2S_MCLK` pinmux commands plus `/opt/sample/audio/sample_audio 2` produced audible output on the MAX98357 speaker.
- Pitfalls: missing ALSA soundcards and missing `aplay` are not blockers for this path. The official sample may print `set inner audio codec ok`; that was present in the successful run. Do not upload MP3 directly to `sample_audio 2`; it expects `audio_chn0.aac` in the current directory.
- Expansion-board diagnosis (2026-07-21): on `EULER_40PEXP`, Pin12/BCLK reaches `J7-6/JTAG_TDO` through U10 and its static-high test produced 3.3 V. Pin38/WS at `J7-7/JTAG_TMS` and Pin40/SD_TX at `J7-9/JTAG_TCK` both remained 0 V during correctly ordered GPIO-high tests. WS and SD_TX share the U3 `RS0104XUTQH12-Q1` level translator, so U3 power, OE, soldering, or the IC itself is the isolated fault area. The resulting BCLK-without-LRCLK condition produced rhythmic speaker thumps instead of audio.
- GPIO-test ordering: sysfs GPIO `export` resets these pad muxes to Fun0 on the current board. Export the GPIO first, then apply the GPIO pinmux value, then set direction/value; otherwise sysfs can report `1` while the physical pad remains low. After testing, unexport and restore `0x102F010C=0x1202`, `0x102F0108=0x1102`, and `0x102F0104=0x1202`.
- Verified competition workaround (2026-07-21): leave the defective J7 outputs disconnected and take the raw I2S signals from the expansion connector input-side J1 pads (`Pin12=BCLK`, `Pin38=WS`, `Pin40=SD_TX`) directly to MAX98357 while keeping the expansion board installed. Static-high tests measured 1.8 V at J1 Pin38 and J1 Pin40, confirming U3 does not clamp the raw inputs. The standard `/root/max98357_i2s_test/audio_chn0.aac` playback then exited with `rc=0`, and the user confirmed normal audible output instead of rhythmic thumps. Power off before future wiring changes.
